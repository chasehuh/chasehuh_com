- We launched `abgcmo.com`, the thing we first came up with at the airport right before leaving the country.
  - It took 10 days from idea to launch.

---

- The schedule slipped more than we expected, and we were worried about that.
- But the reaction turned out to be much bigger than we thought.
  - On day one, it felt roughly half positive and half negative, which seemed like an appropriate level of response.
  - Then at some point, non-tech Twitter started talking about "nuke sf," and the haters multiplied.

---

- Growing while making haters is not exactly beautiful.
- Still, we think it can be one way to win.
  - Of course, we had no intention of ragebaiting anyone.
  - We did not realize ABG culture was rooted that deeply.
  - And since we are Asian ourselves, we did not expect it to get entangled with racism in that way.

---

- Even so, the revenue was meaningful.
  - We hit $50k ARR, not accounting for annual churn lol.
  - And among ourselves, we learned something simple and almost funny: when revenue comes in, runway gets longer. Wow.
